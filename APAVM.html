About the Scheme

The Annasaheb Patil Arthik Vikas Mahamandal provides financial assistance, subsidies, and loans to eligible entrepreneurs from Maharashtra. Our CA firm offers complete support for document preparation and application submission.

Who Can Apply?

  • Residents of Maharashtra
  • Belonging to Economically Backward Class (EBC)
  • New or Existing Business Owners

Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What documents are needed?

Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Caste Certificate, Income Proof, Business Plan, etc.

▶ How much subsidy can I get?

Subsidy amounts depend on your business type, loan amount, and category. Usually 20-30% subsidy is available.

▶ How long does the process take?

Usually 30 to 60 days after document submission and approval.
