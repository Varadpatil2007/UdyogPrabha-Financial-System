Who We Are

UDYOG PRABHA is a Ministry of MSME-supported initiative dedicated to providing professional and transparent Chartered Accountant services to entrepreneurs, startups, and small businesses across India.

From business loans to GST registration and income tax filing, our experts ensure compliance and smooth service delivery to empower India's business community.

Our Mission

To empower MSMEs by offering reliable financial services, business guidance, and digital support through a unified platform.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for India’s small businesses in their journey toward sustainable growth and digital compliance.
