Comprehensive Guide to Agriculture Loan in India

Everything farmers need to know about agri-finance, loan types, eligibility, and interest rates.

Agriculture loans are credit facilities tailored to meet the financial needs of Indian farmers. These loans are critical for crop production, livestock care, irrigation, and agri-infrastructure development.

Types of Agriculture Loans

  • Crop Production Loans (short-term)
  • Investment Loans for machinery & infrastructure
  • Working Capital Loans & overdrafts
  • Hi-Tech Agri Loans like drip irrigation, hydroponics

Banks and NBFCs typically offer agriculture loans at interest rates ranging between 7% and 13.75%. Fixed ROI options are also available under certain government or private schemes.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian citizen aged between 18 and 70 years
  • Engaged in agriculture or agri-allied activities
  • Must not be listed as a CIBIL/RBI defaulter

Agri-loans play a major role in supporting rural development and farmer empowerment. With simplified digital applications, many banks now offer fast approvals and minimal documentation.

Whether you're a small landholder or a commercial farmer, a suitable farm loan can help scale your operations efficiently and sustainably.
