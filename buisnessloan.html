💼 Business Loan Information

Running a business isn’t simple—whether you want to set up a new business or restart an existing one, financial support is essential. Many business owners lack sufficient funds to purchase products or manage day-to-day operations. A business loan can provide the necessary assistance to maintain and grow your enterprise.

The primary purpose of a business loan is to meet urgent financial needs and help take your business to the next level. That’s why it has become a crucial tool for every businessperson.

A business loan from UDYOG PRABHA PVT. LTD. can support your launch or expansion. We understand that businesses require funds for infrastructure, machinery, inventory, and working capital. Our team offers customized loan solutions designed to scale your enterprise.

📌 Loan Categories

Business loans are categorized into:

  • Unsecured Loans – Offered to Pvt Ltd, Ltd, Partnership Firms, and Proprietorships for a maximum term of 3 years.
  • Secured Loans – Provided as working capital in INR or foreign currency for the same entities.


🎯 Business Loan Benefits

  • Minimal paperwork required; some customers may get loans without collateral.
  • Customized loan options available.
  • Flexible tenure from short-term to long-term repayment.
  • Loan processing typically completes in 4 working days.

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant age should be between 25 to 65 years.
  • Business must be profit-making for at least 3 years.
  • At least 1 year of filed Income Tax Returns.
  • Audited balance sheet by a registered Chartered Accountant.

🗂️ Required Documents

  • Identity proof: PAN Card, Aadhar, Voter ID, Passport, etc.
  • Address proof: Utility bill, Rent Agreement, Ration Card, etc.
  • Last 6 months of bank statements.
  • Minimum monthly income of ₹30,000.

💰 Interest Rate

The interest rate depends on several factors including your business plan, loan amount, and repayment duration. Banks evaluate risk and potential while determining the applicable interest rate.
