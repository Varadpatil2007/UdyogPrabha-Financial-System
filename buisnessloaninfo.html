Business Loan Guidance

Everything you need to know before applying for a business loan with UDYOG PRABHA.

Why Choose a Business Loan?

Business loans can help you grow, expand, or stabilize your business operations. Whether it’s for purchasing equipment, hiring staff, or covering working capital, we assist you in getting loans with minimum hassle.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid business registration (Shop Act, Udyam Aadhar)
  • Minimum 1 year of business operations
  • ITR filed for the last 3 years
  • Clean repayment history

Documents Required

  • PAN & Aadhaar Card
  • ITR for 3 years
  • Bank Statement (6 months)
  • Business Registration (Shop Act / Udyam Aadhar)
  • GST Certificate
  • Rental Agreement or Property Card
  • Electricity Bill / 7/12 Utara
  • Detailed Project Report
