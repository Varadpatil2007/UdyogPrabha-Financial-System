Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of these financial tools?

Our tools help you make informed financial decisions by calculating EMIs, interests, SIP returns, and investment values quickly and accurately.

2. Are these calculators free to use?

Yes, all calculators on UdyogPrabha are completely free and can be accessed without signing up.

3. Can I use these tools for business or personal finance?

Absolutely! Our calculators are suitable for both personal and business financial planning including loans, SIPs, and investments.

4. How accurate are the results provided?

Our tools use standard financial formulas. The results are highly accurate for planning purposes, but exact figures may vary slightly with real-time financial institution rates or fees.

5. Can I share or embed these tools on my website?

Currently, tools are designed for use on UdyogPrabha.com, but you can share the links. If you need embedded versions, please contact our team for collaboration.
